The Trading Journal

Where reflection becomes your edge

Every elite trader keeps a journal. Not just for trades — for thoughts, emotions, and patterns. Mental Mods members get access to a structured trading journal designed by a licensed therapist to surface the mental data your charts can't show you.

  • 📝
    Pre & Post-Session Entries

    Log your mindset before the open and debrief after the close. Spot emotional patterns before they cost you.

  • 🧠
    Psych Check-In Prompts

    Clinically-informed prompts designed by Brandon to help you identify fear, ego, and bias in real time.

  • 🔁
    Pattern Recognition Over Time

    Your journal builds a record of your mental tendencies — so you stop making the same mistake twice.

  • 📓
    Consistency Tracking

    Discipline isn't motivation. It's repetition. Your journal holds you accountable to your own process.
